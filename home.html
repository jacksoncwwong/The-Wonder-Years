The Wonder Years
Montessori

A Child's most formative years are their youngest. From birth to age six. These are 'The Wonder Years'.

Ninety percent of the child's intellect, personality and social skills are developed by age six. Children under six absorb information effortlessly, merely by living and interacting in everyday ways. Children are naturally drawn to the things needed to help them to reach their fullest potentials. The Wonder Years develops these tendencies in young children, offering an array of real life scenarios in child-sized versions.
